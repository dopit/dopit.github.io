Work / Life
Balance

This isn’t just another tagline. We’re committed to maintaining a startup’s ambition and flexibility without the grueling hours that keep you from maintaining a healthy family and personal life. We value the quality of the work you put out, not the hours you put in.

Culture

We have a bias towards prototyping, experimenting, and trying things out. Some of our greatest ideas and collaborations are born when we’re playing not working, so we make time to eat lunch and explore NYC together. We don’t have a conference room but we do have a homemade arcade.

Benefits

We want you to have everything you need to be successful. And that means top-notch health benefits and family planning, retirement and investing plans, free lunch, company sponsored conference travel and learning opportunities, team outings, and much more.
